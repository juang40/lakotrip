About Us

Welcome to Lako Trip

Lako Trip stands as a premier local tour and travel agent headquartered in Labuan Bajo, a gateway to the enchanting Flores Island in Indonesia. Established with a vision to bridge local expertise with international service standards, Lako Trip has swiftly gained a reputation for excellence in the travel industry. The company was founded by a group of passionate travel enthusiasts whose extensive backgrounds in tourism and hospitality have been instrumental in shaping the company's direction and ethos.
